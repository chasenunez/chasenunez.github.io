Mega-Gardeners Citations

This project is based on research conducted by an international team of dedicated scientists and field researchers. We are grateful to the US Fish & Wildlife Service and the Agence Nationale des Parcs Nationaux (ANPN) for funding and implementing the forest elephant collaring project, without which this project would have been impossible. We would also like to thank Duke Bass Connections for funding this project and for providing us with the opportunity to pursue this novel and important endeavor. Finally, a sincere thanks to our many partners in Gabon for their support, leadership, and continued friendship.
